About Us

Advisory System for Animal Health
an innovative digital solution developed to revolutionize animal health management. This platform is designed to empower farmers and veterinarians with accessible, reliable, and actionable advice to ensure the well-being of livestock and enhance agricultural productivity.

Our system integrates the power of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and data analytics to deliver personalized health recommendations. It enables users to input animal health data, such as symptoms or case histories, and receive guidance on disease prevention, treatment, and best practices for animal husbandry.

Our Mission

We aim to bridge the gap between animal owners and veterinary services by providing:

  • Timely and Accurate health advice
  • Preventative care reminders, including vaccinations and hygiene tips.
  • A centralized platform for managing animal health records.

Our Actions

We call on governments and international agencies to prioritise strong animal health systems through five pillars for action:

Support community engagement and equitable access to animal health services

Increase the numbers and improve the skills of the animal health workforce

Close the veterinary medicines and vaccines gap

Improve animal disease surveillance

Enhance collaboration for One Health

Core Feautures

We call on governments and international agencies to prioritise strong animal health systems through five pillars for action:

Symptom Checker: Get an initial assessment of animal health conditions based on reported symptoms.

Appointment Scheduling: Book and manage appointments with veterinary experts seamlessly.

Preventive Care Reminders: Stay informed about vaccination schedules, deworming, and other health milestones.

Chatbot-Based Interaction: Experience an easy-to-use conversational interface for quick health advice.

Animal Health Record Management: Maintain a comprehensive database of your livestock's health history.
